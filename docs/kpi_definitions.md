# KPI Definitions

- **Total Requests**: Count of requests in selected period.
- **Open Requestion**: Requests without `closed_date`.
- **Avg Resultion Hours**: Average hours from `created_date` to `closed_date`.
- **SLA Met % (48)**: Share of requests within 48 hours.
- **Backlog 7d**: Open requests with `hours open`> 168.
